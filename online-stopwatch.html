How to Use

  • Start: Begin the stopwatch
  • Pause: Temporarily stop the timer
  • Lap: Record split times without stopping
  • Reset: Clear all times and start over
  • Keyboard Shortcuts: Space (Start/Pause), L (Lap), R (Reset)

Free Online Stopwatch for Precise Time Tracking

Welcome to Precision Stopwatch, a free online tool designed for accurate time measurement. Whether you're tracking workout intervals, cooking times, presentation durations, or scientific experiments, our stopwatch provides reliable timing with millisecond precision.

Features of Our Stopwatch

Our online stopwatch includes lap/split timing functionality, allowing you to record multiple time intervals without resetting. The intuitive interface works on all devices - desktop, tablet, and mobile. With keyboard shortcut support (Space, L, R keys), you can control the timer hands-free during activities.

Practical Applications

This tool is perfect for athletes timing interval training, chefs monitoring cooking processes, teachers conducting classroom activities, presenters rehearsing speeches, and professionals tracking productivity sessions. The lap feature helps compare split times during races or multi-stage activities.

How Stopwatches Work

Digital stopwatches like this one use JavaScript's high-resolution time API to track elapsed time with extreme accuracy. Unlike analog stopwatches, digital versions eliminate human error and provide precise measurements down to milliseconds.
